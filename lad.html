  • Tour Day 1 - Arrival in Leh:

    Airport pickup and transfer to hotel. Full day rest and after lunch at your hotel. Overnight stay at hotel in Leh.

  • Tour Day 2 - Leh - Hemis /Shey /Thiksey:

    Full day visiting Shey, Thiksey. After breakfast, proceed to Shey - it was the ancient capital of Ladakh. The importance which was attached to Shey - the seat of Ladakh's heartland. Overnight at hotel in leh.

  • Tour Day 3 - Pangong Lake:

    Leave early morning for Pangong. Lake through Changla pass 5486 Mtrs. While ascending Changla pass 5486 Mtrs. Enjoy the beautiful panoramic view of the villages below the pass. Arrive Durbuk and from there a short drive takes you to the western shore of the lake. Overnight at hotel in leh.

  • Tour Day 4: Leh - Khardungla Pass + Nubra Valley:

    Half day excursion to Khardongla pass, which is the highest Motorable pass in the world 5600 Mtrs. After noon visit to Samker Gompa, Leh Bazaar/Gompa and Shanti supa.. Samker Gompa is little up in the valley, 3 Kms from Leh Town, which is open to visitors in the morning and evening only. After Samker, visit Leh Monestry/Shanti Stupa. & Walk back or Drive to Leh. Overnight stay in hotel in Leh.

  • Tour Day 5: Full day visit/Alchi/Sangam/Magnetic Hill::

    After breakfast at hotel drive to Alchi, Alchi the most beautiful Gompa of Ladakh also called Jewel among the central Ladakh's religious sites. It's known as Chos-Kor, or religious enclave. On the way stop at magnetic hill, sangam (coinfluence of indus an zanaskar river).Also visit of hall of fame, Gurudwara Pather Sahib. Overnight in Hotel in Leh.

  • Tour Day 6: Leh - Airport:

    After breakfast transfer to Leh Airport to catch flight for onward destination with Sweet holiday memories. Tour Ends
